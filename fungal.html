What is Black Fungus (mucormycosis) ?

Mucormycosis is a very rare infection. It is caused by exposure to mucor mould
which is commonly found in soil, plants, manure, and decaying fruits and vegetables.

It affects the sinuses, the brain and the lungs and can be life-threatening
in diabetic or severely immunocompromised individuals
such as cancer patients or people with HIV/AIDS

know more

Symptoms of mucormycosiscan

  • Headache and nasal obstruction
  • Pain or loss of sensation on Face
  • Discolouration of skin of face
  • One-sided facial swelling
  • Gastrointestinal bleeding
  • Loosening of teeth/dsicoloration of palate

Precautions you can take

  • Recovered patients should take a high protein, low sugar diet, and take Vitamins A, E and B-Complex
  • Nasal irrigation with betadine can help prevent infections
  • Steam inhalation is recommended
  • maintain good oral hygiene
